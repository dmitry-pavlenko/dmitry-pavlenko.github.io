GUIDE SUPPORT

Hi, dear friends!

I’m glad to see you on my page!

Let me to offer my skills and experience in mountain guiding.

I’ve started my climbing  in 1987, and guiding career in 1999.

Like climber I’ve got a great experience in rock climbing, ice climbing, mixt climbing and high altitude climbing.

I took part in two climbs which been awarded  Piolet d’Or. Makalu West face 1997, Jannu North face 2004.

Also I’ve got great experience in solo climbing.

Like guide I’ve got experience in all types of climbing. From rock routes to 8K.

My programs:

Training and climbing programs in Ala-Archa. Kyrgyzstan.

  • Rock climbing training
  • Ice climbing training
  • Mixt climbing training
  • Climb different routes from F to ED
  • Exclusive program climb new routes

Technical climbing in Kyrgyzstan.

  • All Terskey range 5K. Dzygyt peak, Karakolsky peak, Oguz Bashi peak.
  • Exclusive program climb new routes

High altitude climbing. Snow leopard program.

  • Lenin peak
  • Korzenevskaya peak
  • Kommunisma (Somoni) peak
  • Khan-Tengri peak
  • Pobeda peak

Also I can organize high altitude climbing in Nepal, Pakistan, China, India.
